About Milagro

Every year, UVCE hosts MILAGRO, the largest cultural event on campus. Over the course of two days, students from colleges across Bangalore and beyond come together to take part in a wide range of exciting activities and competitions. MILAGRO is incredibly popular, drawing a huge crowd of students each year. Its success is a testament to its widespread appeal and the enthusiasm it generates among attendees. People are captivated by the event and always eager for more. The festival is a celebration of culture and creativity, providing students with a platform to showcase their talents and artistic abilities. From music and dance performances to art exhibitions and theater productions, MILAGRO offers something for everyone. It's more than just a festival; it's a chance for students to express themselves and demonstrate their skills. MILAGRO has become a highlight of the academic calendar, bringing people together and creating lasting memories for all who attend

About UVCE

University of Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE), founded in 1917 by Bharat Ratna Awardee Sir M Visvesvaraya, is A State Autonomous Public University on IIT model that offers B.Tech., B.Arch., M.Tech. (Engineering), and Ph.D. degrees in the many engineering and architectural fields. After 100 years, this pioneering college has cemented its place as one of the most distinguished colleges in the nation which adopted the IIT model of learning in 2022, along with becoming an autonomous institution.With the tireless work of the students, the college hosts a lot of successful technical and non-technical activities. The college takes pleasure in its alumni's successful entrepreneurship endeavours, its students decision to pursue higher education, and the outstanding placements they receive each year
